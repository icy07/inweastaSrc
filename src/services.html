Inweasta provides a wide range of debt resolution services in various jurisdictions of Europe, Asia and America. We always find reliable solutions which will satisfy all parties of debt arguments and will cost less time and funds than a typical lengthy arbitration process.

Our legal team is experienced in providing litigation funding services around the globe for disputes between businesses. We offer flexible and smart solutions tailored to satisfy our partners in each particular situation and litigation.

The management team of Inweasta includes professionals specializing in investment in various fields with over 20 years of experience. We structure complex investment deals in both financial and industrial fields, in start-ups and operational businesses.

Inweasta has a vast portfolio and deep expertise in restructuring businesses in special situations. A restructure may aim at splitting a business between shareholders, attracting new investments or other types of complex deals.

Having legal, financial, investments, security, PR and other competences in-house gives Inweasta a unique ability to work efficiently on complicated projects that require multiple competences being applied.
